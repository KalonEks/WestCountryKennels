Brian Roushar has been training dogs since 1991, developing a lifelong passion for helping dogs thrive both at home and in the field. His approach blends practical experience with a deep understanding of canine behavior, and he sets the standard for the training methods used throughout the Kennel. Under his leadership, the entire training team follows the same philosophy of producing reliable, wellrounded dogs.
