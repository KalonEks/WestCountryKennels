Engaging Environments

Varied Terrain & Engaging Water Work

At West Country Kennels, our 50+ private acres are designed to give every dog, family companion or future hunting partner a safe, structured, and enjoyable place to learn and grow. Our grounds offer a thoughtful mix of open fields, wooded spaces, and varrying transition areas that encourage exploration while supporting training at any level.

Our custom-built ponds are a key part of the experience here. While they’re engineered to support water work for hunting dogs, they’re equally valuable for helping young or family dogs build confidence and develop healthy, positive associations with new environments.
