Technical Environments

Varied Terrain &
Technical Water.

West Country Kennels is situated on sixty private acres featuring heavy cover, upland fields, and manicured transition zones. Our "Technical Water" ponds are the centerpiece of the facility—engineered with specific points, channels, and islands to teach dogs to take lines through water and hold them under pressure.
