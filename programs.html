No Hunting Experience

Puppy Program

Our puppy program introduces dogs to birds (such as pigeons, chukkars pheasants and ducks), firearms and water. Dogs as young as 5 months or dogs with no hunting experience are encouraged to start at this level. This program provides the building blocks in becoming a good hunting dog.

This program will guide your dog through:


  • Introduction To Firearms
  • Live Bird Introduction
  • Flushing (Land & Water)
  • Retrieving (Land & Water)

INTERMEDIATE PROGRAM

The critical foundation for any future Pointer or Retriever. We focus on shaping the mind to build confidence and trust with your dog in the field.

This program will guide your dog through:


  • On-Off Leash Obedience
  • Retrieve To Hand (Force Fetching)
  • Water Skills (Boats, Blinds, Decoys)
  • Steady To Shot
  • Pointing skills
  • Field skills (Flushing, Tracking, Quartering, Flyaways)
